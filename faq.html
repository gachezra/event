Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

  • What is EventKick Kenya?

    EventKick Kenya is a web app designed to help tourists find events and activities to do while visiting Kenya.

  • How do I use EventKick Kenya?

    Simply go to our website and search for events by date, location, or category. You can also create an account to save your favorite events and receive personalized recommendations.

  • Is EventKick Kenya free to use?

    Yes, EventKick Kenya is completely free to use for both tourists and event organizers.

Account Questions

  • How do I create an account?

    Click on the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner of the website and follow the instructions to create your account.

  • What are the benefits of creating an account?

    By creating an account, you can save your favorite events, receive personalized recommendations, and manage your event listings if you are an event organizer.

  • What do I do if I forget my password?

    Click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page and follow the instructions to reset your password.
